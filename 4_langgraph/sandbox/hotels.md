# Recommended Hotels in Toronto

1. **One King West Hotel & Residence**  
   A popular choice for its central location and amenities.  
   **Address:** 1 King St W, Toronto, ON M5H 1A1, Canada
2. **Intercontinental Toronto Centre**  
   Known for luxury and great views of the city, situated close to major attractions.  
   **Address:** 225 Front St W, Toronto, ON M5V 2X3, Canada
3. **Novotel Toronto Centre**  
   Great value with comfortable accommodations in a central setting.  
   **Address:** 45 The Esplanade, Toronto, ON M5E 1W2, Canada
4. **Four Seasons Hotel Toronto**  
   A luxury hotel offering exceptional service and facilities.  
   **Address:** 60 Yorkville Ave, Toronto, ON M4W 0A4, Canada
5. **Shangri-La Hotel Toronto**  
   Known for its opulence and high-end dining options.  
   **Address:** 188 University Ave, Toronto, ON M5H 0A3, Canada
6. **Fairmont Royal York**  
   A historic hotel with elegant decor, located next to Union Station.  
   **Address:** 100 Front St W, Toronto, ON M5J 1E3, Canada
7. **1 Hotel Toronto**  
   Offers a sustainable luxury experience with beautiful views of Lake Ontario.  
   **Address:** 550 Wellington St W, Toronto, ON M5V 2V4, Canada
8. **The Hazelton Hotel**  
   Combines luxury with a boutique feel in one of Toronto's trendy neighborhoods.  
   **Address:** 118 Yorkville Ave, Toronto, ON M5R 1C2, Canada
9. **The Ritz-Carlton, Toronto**  
   Offers impeccable service, luxurious rooms, and a prime location.  
   **Address:** 181 Wellington St W, Toronto, ON M5V 3G7, Canada.
